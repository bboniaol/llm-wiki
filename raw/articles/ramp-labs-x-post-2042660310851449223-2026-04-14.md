This is a long Ramp Labs article arguing that hierarchical multi-agent systems waste tokens by repeatedly passing redundant context. The proposed method, “Latent Briefing,” uses task-guided KV-cache compaction to preserve only context the worker model is likely to need, with reported savings and similar or better benchmark accuracy on LongBench v2.
